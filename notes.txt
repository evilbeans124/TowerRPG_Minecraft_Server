Statistics:
  Strength: Every 5 Strength = With anything other than a SWORD, You attack half a heart more.
  Stamina: Every 5 Stamina = You gain half a heart.
  Dexterity: Every 5 Dexterity = With a BOW ONLY, you attack half a heart more. (1/5 percent chance it will deal 10% more damage)
  
Leveling:
  You start out with:
    0 Stamina: (actually 20)
    0 Strength: (actually 5)
    0 Dexterity: (actually 9)
    
  For every level you increase:
    Strength += 5
    Stamina += 5
    Dexterity += 5
